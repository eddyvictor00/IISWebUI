02 The Problem We Solve

Conventional quantitative trading models train on all available market data — signal and noise alike. The result is a model that learns to predict the average, performing adequately across all market conditions but excelling in none. High-frequency noise is baked into the weights, diluting conviction on genuine high-probability setups.

AII Web's architecture inverts this paradigm. We generate a broad high-frequency signal set, then systematically purge every negative and low-alpha position from the training data. The backpropagation neural network trains exclusively on verified "winning" patterns — the precise technical conditions that historically precede significant profitable price moves.

03 How the Signal Is Generated

Four sequential models, each a stricter quality filter than the last.

TR30
Classifier
Baseline Trend Identification
20-indicator input vector (MACD, RSI, EMA, VWAP). 3-month sliding window over 5-year history. Binary long/short output. Confidence gate — signals suppressed below 0.70.
TR31
Profit Filter
Strategic Profit Filtering & Retraining
Simulates TR30 signals. Parameter sweep maximizes net P&L. Purges all negative-return trades. Retrains backprop NN exclusively on the success-only dataset.
TR32
Conservative
Advanced Capital Preservation Filter
Applies a stricter margin-gap requirement than TR31. Higher pruning percentage. Fires only on setups with significant historical edge — prioritizes capital preservation over trade frequency.
TR33
Executor
Consensus Execution Engine
Dual-layer consensus: best signal from TR31 or TR32. Trend-aligned via TR30. Price boundary check (30%–70% of historical window). Daily full pipeline retraining.
